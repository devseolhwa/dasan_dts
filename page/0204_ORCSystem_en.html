ORC System

Turn Your Waste Heat into a Valuable Asset

Industrial Waste Heat Recovery Solution Based on ORC Technology

Everyday, your factory emits vast amounts of residual heat that simply disappears into the atmosphere—resulting in energy waste and environmental impact.
Our Organic Rankine Cycle (ORC) solution captures this medium- to low-temperature waste heat and converts it into stable, renewable electricity 24/7.
This is the most cost-effective and eco-friendly way to power your operations.
Significantly reduce your energy bills and elevate your company’s ESG performance with DTS’s specialized ORC system.

Why ORC Systems Are Essential for Industrial Sites

Industries with high thermal demand—such as steel, non-ferrous metals, petrochemicals, cement, and glass—release a tremendous amount of waste heat during operations.
Traditional technologies have struggled to utilize heat sources in the 100-300°C range.
However, ORC systems use organic fluid with low boiling points instead of water, allowing turbines to be driven by lower-grade heat sources and producing reliable power without the need for high-pressure steam.

Key Benefits of Deploying an ORC System

  • Drastic Electricity Cost Savings
    Generate power directly from waste heat to significantly reduce or even eliminate the need to purchase electricity from external sources—enhancing energy independence.
  • Powerful ESG Performance & Regulation Compliance
    Produce clean energy without fossil fuels, achieving near-zero greenhouse gas emissions and staying ahead of regulations such as Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanisms (CBAM).
  • 24/7 Continuous Power Generation
    Operates independently of weather conditions, using stable industrial heat flow to supply continuous power all year round.
  • Flexible Load Response
    Operates effectively across a wide range—from as low as 10% to over 105% thermal load conditions.
  • Strong Partial Load Efficiency
    Maintains high generation efficiency even under partial load, minimizing turbine performance loss.
  • Low Maintenance, Simple Operation
    Designed for low-pressure operation and fully automated control, enabling easy management with minimal staffing.

Key Application Industries

DTS’s ORC solution is optimized for converting unstable and low-grade waste heat into reliable power—particularly in high energy-consuming industries pursuing carbon neutrality.

  • Steel / Non-Ferrous Metal Waste heat from reheating furnaces, annealing furnaces, and melting furnaces
  • Petrochemical / Refining Residual heat from distillation columns, heaters, reactors
  • Cement / Glass Exhaust heat from kilns, coolers, and melting furnaces
  • Waste Incineration High-temperature waste heat from municipal and industrial incinerators
  • Other Industries Waste heat from engines, compressors, dryers, and more
